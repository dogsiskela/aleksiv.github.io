$1000 WEEKLY CONTEST RULES

The Vegas Lit Games Free2Play Sportsbook allows you to place bets on real sports with virtual currency and track your progress over time.

After you register, you'll be gifted $1000 in virtual currency each week. Bet on sports over the week using the app.

Earn as much as possible through your correct picks and climb the leaderboard to become the Vegas Lit Champion!

The player with the highest profit each week will be declared the winner and awarded cold hard plastic! (Cash Card)

OFFICIAL CONTEST RULES

1. Players get $500 every Thursday at 6AM PST when the weekly game starts. Each weekly game ends the following Wednesday night at midnight PST.
2. Once you register, you’ll get $500 in virtual currency to play with each week. You can easily see how you did on each bet and also track how much you’re making over time.
3. Players can only participate in the contest with a single SBD Play account.
4. Every week (6AM PST Thursday) player accounts are reset at $500 and the game restarts.
5. Players can choose from a daily list of active games in the NBA, NFL, MLB, or NHL.
6. Players can make moneyline, spread, and over/under bets on any active games during the contest week.
7. The max a player can bet on a single game is $100 and the min is $1.
8. Each player in the contest must make a minimum of 3 bets during the weekly contest to qualify for the winning prize.
9. The max number of picks each day of the contest is 25.
10. The winner is the player with the highest profit after the weekly contest ends at Wednesday midnight PST. In the case of a tie, the player who placed a higher number of correct bets is the winner.
